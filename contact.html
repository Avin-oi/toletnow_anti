❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Is posting a property really free?+
Yes! Posting and browsing properties on ToLetNow.in is 100% free, forever. No hidden charges, no broker fees.
How long will my listing be active?+
Each listing stays live for 30 days. After that, you can re-post for free to keep your property fresh and visible.
How do tenants contact me?+
Tenants call your registered phone number directly. There's no middleman — you negotiate and decide everything yourself.
How do I report a fake listing?+
Use the "Report Listing" button on any property card, or contact us with the listing details and we'll investigate within 24 hours.
Is ToLetNow.in available outside Salem?+
Currently we are focused exclusively on Salem city. We're planning to expand to nearby districts soon. Stay tuned!
